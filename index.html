About us

We are a CSA (BC Certified Organic PACS 16-024) farm. We provide direct access to local, organically grown, seasonal, farm-fresh produce in local markets.

We provide direct access to local, organically grown, seasonal, farm-fresh produce including fruits, berries, greens, melons, root vegetables, legumes, tomatoes, cucumbers, and peppers to name a few. With a wide variety to offer you can find us around the Lower Mainland at local farmers markets.

History

Joseph and Judy Blades are pioneers in BC Certified Organic farming and their beginnings date back to 1996. The farm is located in the semi aired region of Spence's Bridge which boasts one of the friendliest growing climates in Canada.

Mary and Jacob Anderson were growing organic produce on this land decades before anyone had even heard the phrase "Organic Produce". Judy and Joseph have continued on in this tradition with a love for real food grown by Mother Nature.

Location

Our Farm is located on IR#9, just out of Spences Bridge, BC.
